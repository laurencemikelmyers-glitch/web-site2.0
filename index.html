Benefits of Exercise

Why Exercise Is Important

Exercise improves both physical and mental health.

It can boost mood, increase energy, and reduce stress.

Top Benefits of Regular Exercise

  • Builds muscle and strength
  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Enhances mood and mental clarity
  1. Start with simple exercises
  2. Stay consistent each week
  3. Track progress and increase intensity gradually
